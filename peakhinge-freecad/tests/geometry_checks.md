# Geometry Checks

## Pipe pivot
- Does the sleeve fit the bore?
- Does the pipe fit the sleeve?
- Is edge distance adequate?
- Is retention geometry possible without collision?

## Ridge scissor
- Does the fold path clear?
- Does deployed geometry seat cleanly?
- Do lock variants collide with rafters or pivot axis?
- Is there unsafe over-center behavior in the concept geometry?

## Cassette corner
- Is the bearing/load path concept obvious?
- Does hinge geometry conflict with cassette structure?
- Is compression taken in bearing rather than by withdrawal fasteners?
