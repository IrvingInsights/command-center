About Daniel Irving

30+ years leading
mission-driven organizations

Daniel Irving brings three decades of hands-on leadership in experiential education, residential camping, and nonprofit management. He has served as a camp director, program designer, and organizational leader — roles that shaped a consulting practice grounded in what actually works in the field.

Irving Insights Consulting works primarily with nonprofits, camps, and education organizations seeking to strengthen their operational foundation, deepen their values alignment, and build leadership capacity at every level of the organization.

The work is relational, direct, and designed to leave your team more capable — not dependent on outside consultants.
